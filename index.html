Our Core Curriculum

Our five modules are systematically designed to build a strong financial foundation, taking students from basic concepts to real-world application.

MODULE 01

Money: More Than Coins

Mr. PocketEd discovers money is not just cash. He learns where it comes from, its various forms, and how it powers the world around us.

Key Learnings:

  • The origin of money and the evolution from the barter system.
  • Identifying and understanding Indian currency notes and coins.
  • Introduction to the digital economy: UPI, mobile wallets, and cards.

MODULE 02

Piggy Bank to Power Bank

Mrs. PocketEd opens her first digital wallet, explores the power of smart saving habits, and transitions from traditional saving to modern digital apps.

Key Learnings:

  • The magic of compound interest and saving early.
  • Exploring different types of bank accounts for students.
  • Practical guide to using India's top saving and payment apps.

MODULE 03

The Magic of Small Steps

Students start a 7-day budgeting challenge, learning to plan for daily expenses like snacks, school supplies, and even festive gifting for Diwali!

Key Learnings:

  • The 50/30/20 rule adapted for student life.
  • How to track daily income (pocket money) and expenses.
  • Setting and achieving short-term financial goals (e.g., buying a new book).

MODULE 04

The Money Parking Lot

Learn the essentials of the banking ecosystem, including savings accounts, UPI payments, ATM usage, and crucial online banking safety protocols.

Key Learnings:

  • How banks in India operate and the role of the RBI.
  • A practical guide to using an ATM and online banking portals safely.
  • How to identify and protect yourself from common online scams and phishing attempts.

MODULE 05

Marketplace: The Kurukshetra

Mrs. PocketEd enters a simulated market with a budget of â‚¹100, learning to navigate consumer choices by comparing needs versus wants to understand true value.

Key Learnings:

  • Distinguishing between essential needs and aspirational wants.
  • Understanding basic consumer rights in India.
  • A simple introduction to the concepts of stocks and mutual funds.

Empowering the Next Generation

Financial literacy is more than just understanding numbers; it's a passport to freedom, security, and opportunity. In today's rapidly evolving world, the ability to manage money effectively is not a luxuryâ€”it's a fundamental life skill. It empowers individuals to make informed decisions, from managing daily expenses to planning for long-term goals like education, homeownership, and retirement. Without this knowledge, young people are vulnerable to crippling debt, predatory scams, and a lifetime of financial stress. By teaching these skills early, we are not just creating smarter consumers; we are building a generation of confident, responsible, and empowered citizens capable of securing their own futures and strengthening the nation's economy.

At PocketEd, we believe that this education should be accessible, engaging, and relatable. We move beyond textbook definitions to immerse students in real-world scenarios they can understand. Our community-driven approach ensures that learning doesn't happen in isolation. It's a shared journey where students, parents, and educators collaborate, turning complex financial concepts into practical, lifelong habits. This foundation of knowledge is the greatest gift we can give to the architects of tomorrow.
